1. Momentum Confidence: Voice clear, Poise steady, Don't wander eyes

3. Conclusion first: Pyramid Principle, Layer by layer progression

AIDA Model

Attention: Attract audience attention

Interest: Spark audience interest

Desire: Emphasize desire provided by product or service

Action: Attract audience to take action

---

SCQA Model

Situation: Describe situation

Complication: Introduce problem or challenge

Question: Raise key questions

Answer: Provide solution

---

STAR Model

Situation: Describe situation

Task: Explain problem to be solved

Action: Describe solution

Result: Show result
